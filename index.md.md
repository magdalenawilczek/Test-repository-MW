#### How to make a toast with butter and jam ####  

**Introduction**
This is an instruction saying how to make a toast with butter and jam. It is intended for people who have never done it before. 

**Ingredients**
bread, sliced
butter
jam

**Equipment**
toaster
knife
teaspoon

**Preparation**
Estimated time: 1-5 minutes, depending on the browning level.

You can make up to two toasts at a time.
Make sure the toaster is plugged in.

1. Take a slice of bread.
2. Insert the slice of bread into the slot on the top of the toaster. 
3. Select the browning level (1 to 5) on the knob. 
1 is for slightly browned, 5 is for heavily browned. 
4. Push down the lever on the side of the toaster. It should stay there. 
5. Wait for the toast to pop up. The lever will return to its initial position automatically.
6. Take the toast out of the toaster. Be careful as it may be very hot.
7. Use knife to spread butter on the toast.
8. Use teaspoon to put jam on the buttered toast.
